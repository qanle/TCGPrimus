1. error not accept ver (PUT, DELETE)
Update web.config file, include modules>remove name="WebDAVModule" in the <system.webServer>

<system.webServer>
		<modules runAllManagedModulesForAllRequests="false">
			<remove name="WebDAVModule" />
		</modules>
		...
</system.webServer>
